Our Mission & History

Our Mission

To empower students to achieve their full potential and become compassionate, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens. We are dedicated to providing a challenging and supportive learning environment that encourages intellectual curiosity and personal growth.

Our History

Founded in 1973, BEHS NO.1 (PYAY) was established with a vision to create a center for academic excellence in the heart of our community. From our humble beginnings with just 200 students, we have grown into a leading institution serving over 1,500 students, with a rich history of achievement in academics, arts, and athletics.

Our campus has evolved over the years, but our core values of integrity, respect, and community have remained unchanged.
